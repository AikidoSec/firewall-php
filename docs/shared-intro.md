This guide will walk you through installing and setting up Zen Firewall by Aikido for your application. Follow the steps below to protect your application.

Before you continue, create an Aikido account and/or app and generate an API token.

1.	Create your Aikido account (if you do not have one yet).
2.	In the Aikido dashboard, go to Zen.
3.	Click Add app.
4.	Enter a name for your app, then click Generate token.
    ![Create an app and token](./resources/create_app.png)
5.	Copy the token. You will need it in the next step.
